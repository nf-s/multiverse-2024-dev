## Location */}

Multiverse is located at Redbanks in Nugent, near Sorell. The address of the property is 276 Masons Rd Nugent, and you can find it on [Google Maps](https://maps.app.goo.gl/78RhwLM2BN5VEUGD9).

The road is well maintained, and suitable for all types of vehicles. Please drive carefully to the site, and obey all speed limits. Once at Redbanks, follow the signs and drive slowly to the Multiverse site. Have your ticket and ID ready so you can receive your wristband.
